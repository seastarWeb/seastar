Where else can you get 12 months warranty on a used motorcycle?

Here at Seastar Superbikes we have looked carefully at the value we offer within our services. Why do customers come to a dealer? - Quality, Trust, Advice and Peace of Mind!!

With this in mind we worked hard with Olympic Warranties to come up with a 12 month warranty cover on ALL non competition machines under 10 years old that should put your mind completely to rest. The details of the covers offered vary slightly upon the age of the motorcycle concerned. Below are the facts in black & white.

Gold Policy : For bikes under 5 years old - £500 per claim for a 12 month period.
Silver Policy : For bikes over 5 years old - £350 per claim for a 12 month period.
Bronze Policy : For bikes over 10 years old - £350 per claim for a 90 day period.
